How to Write Interior Design Prompts
Creating a good prompt for Interior Design with Midjourney requires a blend of clarity, creativity, and specificity.

Major Points for Creating Effective Prompts:

Be concise: Keep your prompts short and to the point to ensure the AI understands your vision.
Use descriptive language: Incorporate vivid adjectives and adverbs to paint a clear picture of the desired outcome.
Avoid ambiguity: Ensure your prompts are clear and unambiguous to avoid misinterpretation by the AI.
Experiment: Don't hesitate to try different keywords and settings to see what works best.
Use a prompt generator: Utilize tools to help generate ideas and refine your prompts.
Add weights to your prompts: Assign importance to specific elements to guide the AI's focus.
Use negative prompts: Specify what you don't want in the image to refine the results further.
Modify your settings: Adjust parameters like aspect ratio and creative tags to better suit your needs.
