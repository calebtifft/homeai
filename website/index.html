Information we collect

  • Photos you provide — processed only to generate your staged result.
  • Device data — a per-install identifier for history, usage limits, and optional backup.
  • Anonymous session — if online backup is enabled, for private storage tied to your install (no email login required).
  • Purchases — subscription status via Apple, Google, and RevenueCat (not full payment details).

How we use it

  • Generate and display staged images

FAQ

Do I need an account?

No. The app works on your device without sign-in. Optional backup uses an anonymous session.

How are my photos used?

Only to generate your staged image. Not used to train public models.

Is there a free tier?

Yes — a limited number of staging runs per day. Pro removes the limit.

How do I restore a purchase?

Settings → Subscription plans → Restore purchases (via your App Store or Google Play account).
